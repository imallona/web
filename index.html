Research interests

I am mainly interested in gene expression modulation, as it lays the ground for any cell fate. The questions which excite my curiosity the most are: How are gene expression programs coded? and maintained? how do they adapt during development? and disease?

To mine the regulation metastructure genome-wide I'm fond of integrating gene expression and epigenomic data, and to develop methods to do so.
