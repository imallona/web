Open positions (2018/19):

  • With Dr Mark D. Robinson at the Master Program in Biostatistics from UZH:

    Assigning cell types using scRNA-seq data

    In this project, we will we design interpretable machine learning techniques on curated single cell RNA-Seq atlases where cell types are known to learn the cell type-specific features. Our aim is to generate predictive models to label cells from datasets with unknown cell identities, as well as to explore the impact of different sources for technical- and biological variation.
